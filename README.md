FriendChat
FriendChat is a simple chatbot implemented in Python that engages in conversations with users based on predefined rules. It can respond to a variety of questions and prompts, providing different responses based on the context of the conversation.

Features:
1. Greets users and asks if they want to chat.
2. Responds to various questions and prompts.
3. Handles exit commands to end the conversation.

Sample Conversations:
Here are some sample conversations you can have with the chatbot:

User: What's up?
FriendChat: Tell me more about that!
User: Do you believe in aliens?
FriendChat: I'm just here to hang out and chat with you.
User: Tell me about your planet.
FriendChat: My planet is a utopia of diverse organisms.
